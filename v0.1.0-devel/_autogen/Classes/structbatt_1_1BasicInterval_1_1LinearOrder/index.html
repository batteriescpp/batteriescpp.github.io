    Detailed Description🔗

    1
    struct batt::BasicInterval::LinearOrder;

    Partial order on Interval values that corresponds to a strict ordering on a one dimensional number line.

    Any pair of intervals that overlap are considered "equal" under this ordering.

    Requires that the lower bound of the "greater" interval is greater than or equal to the upper bound of the "lesser."
