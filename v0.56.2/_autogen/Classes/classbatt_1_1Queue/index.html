    template <typename Items >
inline Status push_all(
    Items && items
)

    Inserts multiple items atomically into the Queue.

    Queue::push_all guarantees that the passed items will be inserted in the given order, with no other items interposed (via concurrent calls to Queue::push or similar).

    items should be an STL sequence; i.e., something that can be iterated via a for-each loop.
